Coronavirus Symptoms

What are the symptoms of COVID-19?

Here is a look at some of the symptoms associated with novel coronavirus:

  • Fever
  • Dry Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Runny Nose
  • Sore Throat
  • Persistant Pain or pressure in the chest

(in extreme cases)

  • Pneumonia
  • Severe acute respiratory syndrome
  • Kidney Failure
